What is Dear Abby?

Dear Abby is a thing where you can email me (Home Page) questions, lets say about, how to cope with the stress of school, or whatever, and I will answer them on this page right here. I will copy your question from the email and paste it on the webpage along with my answer! I will try to keep up with the emails every day, but I can't garentee your question will be posted the very next day! Anyways, feel free to ask any questions, and I will answer them! Also don't use your real name when you do the from,... part of the letter just use a fake name like, Sea Overlord, or whatever.
